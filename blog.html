AI PHOTO RESTORATION PROJECT

Project Overview

In this project, I was tasked with restoring a series of vintage family photos that had been damaged over time. The client wanted to preserve their family history by enhancing the images while maintaining their original charm.

Client Requirements

  • Restoration of 5 vintage photos that were faded, had scratches, and discoloration.
  • Enhancement of details to improve overall image quality.
  • Preservation of the original aesthetic to retain the nostalgic feel of the photos.

Tools Used

  • Stable Diffusion: For image enhancement and restoration.
  • Adobe Photoshop: For final touch-ups and adjustments.
  • Topaz Labs AI: To upscale and sharpen images.

Process:

Initial Assessment:
Evaluated the condition of each photo and identified areas needing restoration, such as scratches, tears, and color fading.
AI Restoration:
Used Stable Diffusion to restore the images by filling in missing parts and correcting color imbalances. The AI model was prompted with specific instructions to enhance facial features and background details.
Client Review:
Presented the restored images to the client for feedback. Made adjustments based on their input, ensuring their vision was met.
Final Delivery:
Delivered the restored images in high-resolution formats, ready for printing and sharing with family members.

Results

  • Enhanced Image Quality: All five photos showed significant improvement in clarity and detail.
  • Client Satisfaction: The client expressed their gratitude, stating that the restored images exceeded their expectations and brought back cherished memories.
  • Preserved Family History: The project successfully preserved the clientâ€™s family history, allowing them to share these restored images with future generations.

Conclusion

This project showcased my ability to combine traditional photo editing skills with advanced AI technology to achieve remarkable results. The use of AI tools like Stable Diffusion not only sped up the restoration process but also allowed for enhancements that would be challenging to achieve manually. This experience further solidified my passion for AI photo editing and my commitment to helping clients preserve their precious memories.
